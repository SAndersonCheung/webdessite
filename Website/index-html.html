Introduction

The purpose of this website is to provide an intertactive tutorial site to go alongside the deliverance of the CSC1024 content for Website design and construction. This module runs for half a year, with two lectures and two practical sessions a week. The practical sessions will exist to provide a place for students to get direct assistance from demonstrators and lecturers with any work they may have in relation to the module.

This website was written to be as simplistic and easy to read as possible, and was designed with the same values.
